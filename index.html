COFFEE SHOP MENU

Regular Coffees

Americano

A coffee made by diluting espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to drip coffee but with a different flavor profile. Its a popular choice for those who enjoy a milder coffee.

Cappucino

It is made with espresso, hot milk, and steamed milk foam. The traditional cappuccino is one-third espresso, one-third steamed milk, and one-third foam.

Espresso

A concentrated form of coffee made by forcing a small amount of nearly boiling water through finely ground coffee beans. Its known for its rich flavor and strong, aromatic taste.

Irish Coffee

A cocktail consisting of hot coffee, Irish whiskey, sugar, and topped with cream. Its typically served in a glass mug and is a popular after-dinner drink.

Lavender Latte

Made with espresso, steamed milk, and lavender syrup. The lavender syrup adds a floral and slightly sweet flavor to the latte, creating a unique and aromatic beverage.

Long Black

Made by pouring a double shot of espresso over hot water, giving it a similar strength to an Americano but with a different flavor profile.

Ristretto

A short shot of espresso made with the same amount of coffee but extracted with less water, resulting in a more concentrated and intense flavor compared to a regular espresso.


Specialty Coffees


Mocha

Also known as a mochaccino, is a delicious combination of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup or cocoa powder, often topped with whipped cream. Its a delightful blend of coffee and chocolate flavors.

Caramel Macchiato

A popular coffee drink made with espresso, steamed milk, and caramel syrup. Its typically prepared by adding the espresso to the milk and caramel syrup, creating a layered look with the espresso on top.

Affogato

A simple and delicious Italian dessert that consists of a scoop of vanilla ice cream or gelato "drowned" in a shot of hot espresso. The hot espresso melts the ice cream, creating a delightful mix of coffee and creamy flavors.

Cafê au Lait

A French coffee drink made with equal parts of hot coffee and steamed milk. Its similar to a latte but typically made with brewed coffee instead of espresso.

Cafê Latte Macchiato

A layered coffee drink made with a shot of espresso and steamed milk. The espresso is "stained" or "marked" with the milk, creating a layered look with the espresso on top of the milk.

Cafê Con Leche

A Spanish coffee drink made with equal parts of strong coffee and scalded milk. Its similar to a café au lait but with a stronger coffee flavor.


Other Drinks


Tea

A beverage made by steeping dried tea leaves in hot water. It comes in many varieties, including black, green, oolong, and herbal, each with its own flavor and health benefits.

Hot Chocolate

A warm beverage made from cocoa powder, sugar, and milk. Its a comforting and sweet drink, often enjoyed during colder months or as a treat.

Iced Coffee

A cold beverage made by brewing coffee and then chilling it. Its typically served over ice and can be sweetened or flavored according to preference.

Frappe

A blended iced coffee drink made with coffee, milk, sugar, and ice. Its often topped with whipped cream and flavored syrups.

Iced Tea

A refreshing beverage made by steeping tea leaves in cold water and then chilling the mixture. It can be sweetened and flavored in various ways, making it a popular drink during hot weather.

Lemonade

A sweet and tangy drink made from lemon juice, water, and sugar. Its a popular and refreshing beverage, especially during the summer months.

Fruit Smoothie

A blended drink made from fruits, typically mixed with yogurt, milk, or fruit juice. Its a nutritious and refreshing beverage, often enjoyed as a snack or breakfast.
